# La Liga 2025/26 TV Revenue Distribution Analysis

**Data Analysis Project** exploring the merit-based prize money distribution and estimated total TV revenue per club.

##  Objective
Analyze La Liga's revenue model equal share vs merit vs commercial revenue.

##  Key Findings
- Top 4 clubs receive ~55% of the merit pool
- Barcelona & Real Madrid dominate total TV revenue due to commercial slice
- Strong equal-share model protects smaller clubs

##  Project Structure
- `data/` → Raw and processed datasets
- `visualizations/` → Generated charts
- `notebooks/` → Jupyter analysis
